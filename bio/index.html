Who I am

Hi! I am a third-year Ph.D. student at University Paris-Saclay and University Evry, currently conducting research in multi-agent systems, event-triggered control, and fault-tolerant control supervised by Prof. M. Chadli at the Lab.IBISC. My primary research interests lie in the multi-disciplinary engineering of complex autonomous systems using control theory and artificial intelligence.
